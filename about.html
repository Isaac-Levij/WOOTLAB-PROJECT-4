Code Minds

Code Minds" is an innovative program aimed at empowering students and National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) interns with essential tech skills. This initiative recognizes the growing importance of technology in today's world and seeks to bridge the gap between traditional education and the demands of the digital age. The program offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip participants with practical knowledge and hands-on experience in various areas of technology. From programming languages like Python and JavaScript to web development, data science, and artificial intelligence, Code Minds covers a wide range of topics relevant to today's tech industry.
